It is envisioned that many of the existing corporate customers that purchase titles from the bookstore will extend their purchases to other items such as stationary and office equipment. The platform will provide corporate reports that details the sales over a chosen period and will include details such as:<br/><br/><ul>
<li>Items Purchased</li><li>Purchase Dates</li><li>Tax Rates</li><li>Discounts</li><li>Authority</li></ul>
<br/>The reports will be disseminated based on the customers' preferences and will include post, email, sales portal. <br/>
