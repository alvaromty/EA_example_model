<b>Diagram Overview - </b> <b>Outsourced Team</b><br/>This deployment diagram describes the deployment scenario of an Outsourced Team. The Central Office relies upon workers who either work <b>remotely</b> or who provide services from one or more <b>consultancies </b>located in other countries. Some individuals and organization connect by a secure connection across the Internet, others connect via a Virtual Private Network on a WAN.<br/><br/>The Pro Cloud Server unlocks the models that were traditionally only accessed by staff in the Central Office and via an EA Client to anyone with a Web Browser, an Internet connection and the permission to access the content. <br/>
