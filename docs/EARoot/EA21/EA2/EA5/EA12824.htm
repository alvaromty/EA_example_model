<b>Diagram Overview - Small Team</b><br/>This deployment diagram describes the deployment scenario of a Standard Development Organization where other distributed individuals and organizations contribute to the review, testing and documenting of the standard. The reusable asset server is a typically a key component in this solution. A wide range of organizations (small and large) will typically adopt the standard and their early feedback is critical to refining the standard. WebEA will provide a useful interface to view the standard and to post discussions and raise issues and change requests.<br/>
