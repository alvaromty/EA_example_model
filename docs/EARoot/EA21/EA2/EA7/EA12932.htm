<b>Diagram Overview - Consulting Office</b><br/>This deployment diagram describes the deployment scenario of a Consulting Office. Staff are often working at customers' sites but the CEO prefers them to spend at least one day a week back at the consultancy office as this encourages team building and cross pollination of ideas.<br/><br/>The work is often unpredictable and the consultancy needs to use freelance resource for a variety of roles from Business Analysis to Developers. WebEA allows all the teams to stay in touch, work on their customers' sites and for freelancers and others to contribute to the models.<br/>
