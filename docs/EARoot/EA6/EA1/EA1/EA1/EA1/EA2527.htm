Ships subject to Title II Part II and Part III of the Communications Act of 1934, as amended have to fit GMDSS equipment under FCC Regulation 47 CFR 80 Subpart W. These include all ships, including fishing vessels, to be navigated in the open sea outside of a harbor or port, except:<br/>Ships other than passenger vessels less than 300 gross tonnage,<br/>Passenger ships having six passengers or less,<br/>U.S. government ships,<br/>Yachts of less than 600 gross tons,<br/>Vessels in tow,<br/>Ships navigating solely on any bays, sounds, rivers or protected waters within the U.S.,<br/>Ships being navigated within the Great Lakes of North America, and<br/>Small passenger ships meeting the requirements of 47 CFR 80 Subpart S.<br/>
