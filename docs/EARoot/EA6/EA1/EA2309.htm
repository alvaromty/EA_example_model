ArchiMate® is an open-standard, enterprise architecture language from The Open Group, based on the IEEE 1471 standard.  It offers a common language for describing the construction and operation of business processes, organizational structures, information flows, IT systems and technical infrastructure, that can be used by Enterprise Architects to describe, analyze and clearly visualize, the relationships between business domains.<br/><br/>A number of examples from The Open Group's 'ArchiMate ® 3.0 Specification' have been replicated here, to illustrate how ArchiMate 3 can be used to model your Enterprise Architecture.<br/><br/>The diagrams for these examples can be accessed using the buttons below.<br/><br/>
