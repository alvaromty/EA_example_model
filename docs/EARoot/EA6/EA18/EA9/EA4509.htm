The Strategy Map extends the Balanced Scorecard approach in order to clarify corporate strategy, using four Perspectives and their 'cause and effect' relationship. <br/>The four Perspectives are:<br/><ul>
<li>Financial</li><li>Customer</li><li>Internal Business Process</li><li>Learning and Growth</li></ul>
Strategy Maps are typically used for planning and communicating the overall strategy of an organization.  Strategy Maps provide a greater emphasis on measuring and managing the value created by enhancing an organization's intangible assets.<br/>To create a new Strategy Map diagram, from the ribbon, select the option:<br/>'Design #gt; Diagram #gt; Add #gt; Strategic Modeling #gt; Strategy Map'.<br/>Here are three examples of different styles of Strategy Map.<br/><br/>
