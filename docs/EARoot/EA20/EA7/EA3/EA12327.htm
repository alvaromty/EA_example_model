The <b>'Play Song'</b> StateMachine is called by the <b>On</b> state of the '<b>Play List'</b> StateMachine.  <br/>It looks up the song named in the variable <b>currentSong</b> and loops for the length specified by the song.  <br/>Depending on the value of the <b>Repeat Mode</b> concurrent state, it may also repeat the song until externally stopped.<br/><br/>Execution of this StateMachine may be interrupted at any time by sending the <b>Stop</b> or <b>PowerOff</b> signals.<br/><br/>For convenience the <b>Load Song</b> action has been added to allow additional songs to be defined.<br/>
