Appears In: Model Mail
Most initiatives are carried out by a team of people, and critical to their success is good communication. Model Mail is a mail system internal to the Enterprise Architect repository that allows users to send and receive mail messages.  One of the important advantages that Model Mail provides, is the ability to embed hyperlinks to model elements, allowing the recipient to click through to diagrams, elements, Packages and reviews that are the subject of the mail.<br/>A typical scenario might see one team member sending a message containing a hyperlink to a set of Requirements, asking other team members for their comments.<br/>Model Mail provides options for inserting hyperlinks to a variety of repository items including: Diagrams, Matrices, Images, Searches, Help Topics, Attributes, Operations and Team Reviews.<br/>Model Mail can be accessed from the 'Start' ribbon by selecting the option:  'Collaborate #gt; Mail'.<br/>
