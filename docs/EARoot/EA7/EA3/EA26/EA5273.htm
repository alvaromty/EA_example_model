Appears In: Visual Filters
Visual Filters provide a mechanism for filtering out parts of a diagram or list of elements, based on the values of selected properties.  Filters can be defined for elements or connectors and there are a wide range of criteria that can be set.  By using filters, you can modify the display of diagrams, dimming, hiding or even selecting components that meet specific criteria,  thereby highlighting certain aspects of the diagram, without affecting the structure and integrity of your model. <br/><br/>Project stakeholders typically have different interests and often only part of a diagram or element list will be relevant to them. Visual filters allow you to present a single diagram in many different ways, by highlighting the elements and connectors that are of interest to specific target audiences.<br/><br/>Filters are stored as part of the model, so a filter you create is available to other users of the model; however, use of a filter is specific to the user, so your use of a filter does not affect another user's view of the diagram.<br/><br/>To access the Filters & Layers window, from the 'Layout' ribbon, select the option:  'Tools #gt; Filters and Layers'.  <br/>This image shows an example of the Filters & Layers window.<br/><br/>
