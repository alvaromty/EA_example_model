The Domain Model (or Business Object Model) is a powerful mechanism for describing the important terms of the business.  It provides a single definition of the terms and their relationships, that is accessible to all project staff.   It offers the advantage that the terms are modeled as elements, allowing them to be linked to other model elements.  Where other model elements refer to Domain Model terms, as may happen in the notes of  a Requirement element, hyperlinks can be created, that link directly to the definition of the term in the Domain Model.<br/><br/>
