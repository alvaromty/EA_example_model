<b>Steps to Reproduce</b><br/><ol>
<li>Create a diagram of your choice for example a Class Diagram</li><li>Bring up the properties docked window for the diagram Ribbon: Start -#gt; Properties</li><li>Enable the Custom Style under the Apperance heading in the properties</li><li>Create three classes</li><li>Select them all, right click and set their drawing style to Elipse</li><li>Select them all, right click and set their Opacity to 50%</li><li>Increase the size of the circles to the desired amount and overlap them accordingly</li></ol><p/>
